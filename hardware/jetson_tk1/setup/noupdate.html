Prepairng without internet and updating

You can easily get desktop without updating system. But your system is old.

Requirement:

Instruction:

  1. Login to Jetson
  2. Read "README.txt" of installer
    3. cd NVIDIA-INSTALLER
cat README.txt
  3. Execute installer and reboot
    4. sudo ./installer.sh
sudo reboot
  4. If a HDMI display is connected to your Jetson tk1, you will get desktop.
